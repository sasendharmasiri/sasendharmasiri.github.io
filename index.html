About Me
Designing Is My Passion

I’ve been designing games since I was 12, driven by my love for gaming and creating immersive experiences. I’ve published a PC game and worked on two projects, gaining skills in Unity, Unreal Engine, C#, Blender, and more. I’m all about creativity, teamwork, and delivering the best possible user experience. Games I’ve played and my journey in game development inspire everything I create. I’m excited to bring fresh ideas and passion to my first professional role in game design.
